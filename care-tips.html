Care Tips 🌱

Keeping your green friends happy and thriving

☀️ Light & Placement

Most indoor plants love indirect sunlight. Keep Tulsi and Aloe near balconies or windows.

💧 Watering

Overwatering kills more plants than neglect. Water only when soil feels dry.

🌱 Soil & Drainage

Use loose, well-drained soil. Add compost for nutrients and avoid water logging.

🍂 Seasonal Care

Protect from harsh winter winds & summer heat. Mist leaves to maintain humidity.

🪴 Repotting

Shift to bigger pots every 1–2 years for healthy root growth.
