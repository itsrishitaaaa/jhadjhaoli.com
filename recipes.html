Tulsi Calm Tea

Soothing tea for stress relief with Tulsi, ginger, and honey.

Ingredients:

  • 5 Tulsi leaves
  • 1 inch ginger
  • Honey to taste

Steps:

Boil water, add Tulsi and ginger, simmer for 5 min. Strain, add honey. Sip warm.

  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Wash the orchid petals gently and pat dry.
  2. Combine sugar and water in a saucepan. Heat until sugar dissolves completely.
  3. Add the orchid petals and simmer on low heat for 10‚Äì15 minutes.
  4. Add lemon juice and stir well.
