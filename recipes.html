Tulsi Calm Tea

Soothing tea for stress relief with Tulsi, ginger, and honey.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp Tulsi leaves
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp honey

Instructions:

Boil water, add Tulsi and ginger, simmer 5 min, strain, add honey, and enjoy warm.

DIY Aloe Vera Gel

Natural soothing gel from your own aloe plant.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Aloe Vera leaf

Instructions:

Peel leaf, scoop gel, blend until smooth. Store in a clean container.

Rose Petal Tea

Refreshing tea with rose petals and subtle honey flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp dried rose petals
  • 1 tsp honey

Instructions:

Boil water, add rose petals, steep 5 min, strain, add honey, and serve warm.

Lemon Ginger Detox

Boosts immunity and helps detox naturally.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions:

Mix all ingredients, drink in the morning on an empty stomach for detox benefits.

Honey Mint Lemonade

Sweet and refreshing lemonade with mint infusion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 5-6 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions:

Muddle mint leaves, add water, lemon, honey, stir, and serve chilled.
