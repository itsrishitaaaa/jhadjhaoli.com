Tulsi Calm Tea

Soothing tea for stress relief with Tulsi, ginger, and honey.

Ingredients:

  • 5 Tulsi leaves
  • 1 inch ginger
  • Honey to taste

Steps:

Boil water, add Tulsi and ginger, simmer for 5 min. Strain, add honey. Sip warm.

DIY Aloe Vera Gel

Natural soothing gel from your own aloe plant.

Steps:

Cut Aloe Vera leaf, scoop gel, blend until smooth. Store in fridge for 1 week.
